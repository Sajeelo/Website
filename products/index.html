Ecosystem

Everything Works Together

Our products are designed as an integrated ecosystem. Data flows seamlessly between modules so you get a unified view of your entire business.

SFA ↔ Inventory

Field orders automatically update stock levels. Sales reps see real-time availability before placing orders.

Projects ↔ Accounting

Project expenses and time logs flow directly into your accounting module for real-time budget tracking.

POS ↔ Inventory

Every sale at the counter instantly deducts from inventory. No manual stock updates needed.

Procurement ↔ Accounting

Purchase orders, goods receipts, and vendor payments are automatically reflected in your financials.

HR ↔ Accounting

Payroll processing generates journal entries automatically. Salary disbursements reconcile seamlessly.

Chat ↔ Everything

Get notified in Team Chat when orders arrive, tasks are assigned, or approvals are needed. Stay informed instantly.

E-Comm ↔ ERP

Online orders instantly reserve stock and flow into the ERP for fulfillment and accounting without manual duplicate entry.

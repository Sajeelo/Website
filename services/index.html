Sales Force Automation (SFA)

Our premier service — a comprehensive mobile-first platform that transforms how your sales team operates in the field. Complete with GPS tracking, intelligent route optimization, and real-time analytics.

  • Mobile Order Capture — Take orders on-the-go with offline support and automatic sync
  • Route Planning & GPS — Optimize field visits with intelligent route suggestions and live tracking
  • Attendance & Day Management — Clock in/out with geo-fencing, track work hours automatically
  • Target & KPI Dashboard — Real-time performance metrics for sales reps and managers
  • Collection Tracking — Record and monitor payment collections with receipt generation
